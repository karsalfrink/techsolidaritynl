Tech Solidarity NL meetup #15

Our next meetup happens Monday, October 1 at Sensor Lab from 19:00–21:00 (doors open 18:30) and is about value sensitive design tools.

We are continuing our investigation of value sensitive design with a deep dive into several tools that help to put human values at the heart of the design process of technology.

In previous meetups, our community has identified several ways of bringing ideas from value sensitive design into a typical UX process. These include maps of indirect stakeholders and their values, and scenarios about a technology’s value impact over longer time frames and at larger scales of adoption.

To kick off this meetup we will introduce these tool ideas. After which, all of us will work together to apply them to design problems we are working on right now, to see how they can help us, and how they might be developed further.

For reports of our previous meetups on value sensitive design, see:

  • Meetup #10, Value Sensitive Design
  • Meetup #14, Practicing Value Sensitive Design
