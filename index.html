Tech Solidarity NL

Tech Solidarity NL is a grassroots community of tech workers in the Netherlands advancing the design and development of more just and egalitarian technology.

Past meetups

In past meetups we have delved into the root causes of the issues that concern us. We have explored how to bring about systemic change through direct interventions as well as contributions to other organizations. Currently we are focusing our efforts on educating and organizing tech workers who want to create positive impact through their day-to-day work.

Organisation

We are a semi-open group of tech workers. Our members are chiefly designers and developers but also journalists, academics et cetera.

Coordinating our efforts are: Kars Alfrink, Iskander Smit, and Rob Maijers. To reach us, simply email info@techsolidarity.nl.

We are inspired by but not affiliated with Tech Solidarity US.
